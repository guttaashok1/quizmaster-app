Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Effective immediately

Short version: Your resume and job description text are only ever used to generate your answer in the moment — they are never stored on our servers. We store only your account credentials (username, hashed password) and daily question counts.

1. Who We Are

Interview Coach ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered interview practice tool built and operated by Ashok Kumar Gutta. We are reachable at support@interviewcoach.ai.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to run the service:

  • Account data — username, email address, and a securely hashed password (PBKDF2, 100 000 iterations). Plaintext passwords are never stored.
  • Usage data — a daily count of AI questions you've asked, used to enforce the free-tier limit of 10 questions per day.
  • Payment data — if you upgrade to Pro, we store your Stripe customer ID and subscription ID. Card details are processed entirely by Stripe; we never see or store them.
  • Session data — a signed JWT stored in your browser's localStorage. It expires after 7 days.

3. What We Do NOT Collect or Store

  • Your resume text or job description — these are sent to the AI only to generate your answer and are immediately discarded.
  • Your voice recordings — audio is transcribed server-side (via OpenAI Whisper) and the raw audio is discarded immediately after transcription.
  • Your interview questions or AI-generated answers.
  • Any tracking cookies or advertising identifiers.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • To enforce the free-tier daily question limit.
  • To manage your Pro subscription via Stripe.
  • To send transactional emails (e.g. password reset). We do not send marketing emails.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude — generates interview answers. Your resume + job description + question are sent to Anthropic's API per request. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI Whisper (optional) — transcribes voice recordings. Audio is not retained by OpenAI beyond the API call. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — processes payments. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — hosts our PostgreSQL database (user accounts, quotas). Data is stored in the US East region. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — hosts the application. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

Account data is retained until you delete your account. Daily question counts older than 90 days are automatically purged. Password reset tokens expire after 1 hour and are marked as used immediately on redemption.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@interviewcoach.ai with the subject line "Data Request". We respond within 30 days.

8. Security

Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2-SHA256 with 100 000 iterations and a unique random salt per user. All data in transit is encrypted via TLS. We use timing-safe comparison for all password checks to prevent timing attacks.

9. Children

Interview Coach is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision. Continued use of the service after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

Questions or concerns? Email support@interviewcoach.ai.
