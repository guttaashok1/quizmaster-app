About Interview Coach

A free, AI-powered tool that helps job seekers practice answering interview questions out loud — with real-time, resume-aware coaching.

What is Interview Coach?

Interview Coach is a browser-based application that combines voice recognition with Claude AI to deliver live, spoken interview coaching. You upload your resume, paste a job description, and simply speak any interview question aloud — the AI responds with a tailored, confident answer in real time, word by word.

Unlike static Q&A lists or scripted mock interviews, Interview Coach adapts every answer to your actual experience and the specific role you're applying for. It uses the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for behavioral questions and keeps answers concise and natural — the way you'd actually say them in a room.

Why we built it

Interviews are one of the highest-stakes moments in a career, yet most people prepare by reading bullet points or watching YouTube videos. The gap between knowing an answer and being able to say it confidently is huge — and the only way to close that gap is to practice speaking out loud.

We built Interview Coach to make that kind of practice accessible to everyone. No expensive coaching sessions, no scheduling conflicts, no embarrassment. Just you, your mic, and an AI that knows your resume.

Privacy

We take privacy seriously. Your resume text and job description are only transmitted to our server when you ask a question, and they are never stored — they exist only for the duration of your browser session. Account data (username, email, hashed password) is stored locally in your browser.

Answers are generated via the Anthropic Claude API. No personally identifiable information is retained by us or shared with third parties beyond what's necessary to generate the response.
