Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, monthly subscriptions cancel instantly. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period with no questions asked.
What counts as a question?
Each time you speak and get an AI answer counts as one question. Stopping and re-starting the recording before an answer is generated does not count.
Is my data safe?
Resume and job description text are only sent during your active session to generate answers — never stored on our servers. Your account data lives in your browser's local storage.
